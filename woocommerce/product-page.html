Accept payments with DePay

Accept thousands of different tokens on multiple blockchains with on-the-fly conversion. Process them directly on your WooCommerce store on-site, and receive them directly into your crypto wallet.

  • Worldwide

  • BNB Smart Chain
  • Polygon

Supported Wallets

  • MetaMask
  • Coinbase
  • Trust Wallet
  • DeFi Wallet by crypto.com
  • 1inch Wallet
  • 100+ additional wallets via WalletConnect

Supported Tokens

All* ERC20 tokens on Ethereum, All* BEP20 tokens on BSC, All* ERC20 tokens on Polygon

*if the token standard is strictly adhered to and a liquidity pool with at least $10'000 in reserves exists for the supported decentralized exchanges (Uniswap v2, PancakeSwap, Quickswap)


What are the benefits for me?

  • No gateway, no redirect: Receive Web3 Payments directly on your WooCommerce store.
  • Immediate cash flow: Receive payments directly into your cryptocurrency wallet - settled immediately.
  • You decide: Receive the tokens you want like Stablecoins, ETH, BNB, MATIC etc.
  • Automatic conversion: Accept thousands of different tokens from your customers.
  • Multichain support: Accept payments on multiple blockchains in parallel like Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, Polygon etc.
  • Muli-Wallet support: Accept payments done with over 100+ of different cryptocurrency wallets like MetaMask, Coinbase, Trust, crypto.com DeFi, 1inch etc.
  • Mobile support: Support crypto currency payments on mobile devices also via WalletConnect QR codes.
  • Peer-to-peer: Payments are made directly into your cryptocurrency wallet. No intermediary holds your funds at any time.
  • No hassle: Payments settle immediately. No fraud, no chargebacks.

Mobile Web3 Payments

Also on a mobile device, your customer's payment experience remains seamless.

Get started with Web3 Payments now:

  1. Add DePay Payments to your cart and check out.
  2. Download, install, and activate the extension in your WooCommerce store.
  3. Once the extension is active, click DePay in your storeâ€™s WP Admin.
  4. Connect your wallet and select the tokens that you want to receive as payment.
  5. Make sure you click "Save settings" to store your configuration and activate the payment option.
  6. That's it. You now receive payments directly into your wallet.
