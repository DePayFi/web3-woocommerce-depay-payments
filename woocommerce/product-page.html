Get started with Web3 payments now:

  1. Add DePay Payments to your cart and check out.
  2. Download, install, and activate the extension in your WooCommerce store.
  3. Once the extension is active, click DePay in your store’s WP Admin.
  4. Connect your wallet and select the tokens you want to receive as payment.
  5. Make sure you click Save settings to store your configuration and activate payment options.
  6. After that step, your setup will be complete. You will receive payments directly into your wallet.
